How It Works

1

Join the chat

Weekly venue announcements, last-minute updates, and host support all happen inside the Zalo and WhatsApp groups.

2

Prep with the topic

Each week includes three conversation patterns, example prompts, and a short word list that people can use immediately from a phone screen.

3

Rotate and practice

Tables are mixed intentionally so locals and international members both get useful speaking time instead of one drifting conversation.
