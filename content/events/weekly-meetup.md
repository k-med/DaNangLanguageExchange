Join us for our weekly language exchange! 

## Format
- **7:00 PM - 7:30 PM:** Arrivals, grab a drink, open mingling.
- **7:30 PM - 8:15 PM:** English conversation practice using our weekly topic.
- **8:15 PM - 9:00 PM:** Vietnamese conversation practice using our weekly topic.

## What to bring
- A notebook and pen if you like taking notes.
- Money for your own drink (there is no entrance fee, just buy a drink to support the cafe!).
- A positive attitude! 

**Note for beginners:** Don't be shy! We have specific groups and resources designed to help you.
