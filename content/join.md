## Who This Is For
We are a community of **serious learners** who want to practice conversational Vietnamese and English in a structured, friendly environment. Whether you are a complete beginner or fluent, you are welcome here.

<div class="expectations-box" style="background: var(--entry); padding: 1.5rem; border-left: 4px solid var(--primary); border-radius: 4px; margin: 2rem 0;">
  <h3>What to Expect at Your First Meetup</h3>
  <ul style="margin-bottom: 0;">
    <li><strong>Learning-Focused:</strong> We are here to practice languages, not just drink coffee.</li>
    <li><strong>Small-Group Rotations:</strong> We split into small tables so everyone gets a chance to speak.</li>
    <li><strong>Vietnamese + English Balance:</strong> We dedicate half the time strictly to English, and the other half strictly to Vietnamese.</li>
    <li><strong>Beginners Welcome:</strong> Don't be shy! We provide vocabulary and questions to help.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
