## How We Coordinate

We use **Zalo** and **WhatsApp** to keep our community connected, announce events, and share resources. 

### 1. Zalo Community (Best for Locals and Long-term Expats)
Zalo is the most popular app in Vietnam. We use our Zalo group for local coordination, event RSVPing, and sharing Vietnamese learning tips.

## What Happens Next?
Once you join the chat:
1. **Introduce yourself!** Let us know what languages you speak and what you're learning.
2. **Check the pinned messages** for details on the upcoming language exchange event.
3. **Show up!** Bring a notebook if you like to take notes, and don't worry about your language level. Beginners are completely welcome.

To prepare for your first meetup, you can also browse our [Topics](/topics) or download [Resources](/resources) in advance!
