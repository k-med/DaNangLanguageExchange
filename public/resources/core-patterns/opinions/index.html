Conversation Patterns

Template

Theo [Person], [Statement]
Literal English

According to [Person], [Statement]

Practice question Theo bạn, [Topic] thế nào?

Examples

  • Theo tôi, Đà Nẵng rất đẹp. In my opinion, Da Nang is very beautiful.
  • Theo tôi, tiếng Việt khó nhưng thú vị. In my opinion, Vietnamese is difficult but interesting.

Prompts

  • Theo bạn, Đà Nẵng thế nào? What do you think of Da Nang?
  • Theo bạn, học ở quán cà phê có tốt không? In your opinion, is studying in a cafe good?
  • Theo bạn, món nào dễ giới thiệu cho người mới? Which dish do you think is easy to introduce to a newcomer?

Opinion frames help hosts keep tables alive with quick follow-up questions like why, really, and for example.
