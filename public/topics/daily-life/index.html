Daily Life

An easy topic for routines, feelings, and simple comparisons from ordinary life.

Topic Pack

Daily life pages are useful when the room includes many first-time members who need immediate speaking confidence.

Use this at the meetup: each table takes these three patterns, speaks for 20 minutes, then rotates.

Patterns Used Today

Habitual Events Mỗi khi [Event], [Action] Whenever [Event], [Action] Feelings [Subject] cảm thấy [Feeling] khi [Situation] [Subject] feels [Feeling] when [Situation] Preference [Subject] thích [A] hơn [B] [Subject] likes [A] more than [B]

Example Prompts

  • Mỗi khi rảnh, bạn thường làm gì? When you are free, what do you usually do?
  • Bạn cảm thấy thế nào khi học lâu? How do you feel when you study for a long time?
  • Bạn thích sống gần biển hay gần trung tâm hơn? Do you prefer living near the beach or near the city center?
  • Buổi tối bạn thường làm gì để thư giãn? What do you usually do in the evening to relax?

Vocabulary

thói quen habit sáng morning tối evening rộn busy mệt tired thư giãn relax đi bộ walk ở nhà stay at home

Meetup Flow

  • Habitual Events
  • Feelings
  • Preference

Rotate groups every 20 minutes. Run one Vietnamese-only round and one English-only round.

Host Prompts

  • Lúc nào là lúc tốt nhất trong ngày của bạn? What is the best part of your day?
  • Tại sao bạn thích điều đó hơn? Why do you prefer that?
  • Có ví dụ nào cụ thể không? Do you have a specific example?
Pro tip

Bring a small notebook

  1. Read the topic page before the meetup.
  2. Copy the patterns into your notebook.
  3. Write three example sentences before you arrive.

You will speak more confidently during the exchange.
