What Makes This Club Different?

We aren't just another casual expat hangout. We actually practice languages.

Focused Practice

We dedicate specific time blocks exclusively to Vietnamese and English, ensuring equal practice for everyone.

Small Group Rotations

No loud, chaotic tables. We split into small groups of 3-4 so you actually get time to speak.

Beginner Support

We provide weekly topic worksheets with vocabulary and icebreakers so you're never stuck for words.
