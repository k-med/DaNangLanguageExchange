### 📚 Beginner Vocabulary
*Use these words to build simple sentences.*

- **ngon** - delicious 
- **ăn** - to eat
- **uống** - to drink
- **món ăn** - dish / food
- **nhà hàng** - restaurant
- **cay** - spicy / hot

### 🟢 Beginner Questions
*Focus on short, simple answers.*

1. What is your favorite Vietnamese food?
2. Do you like cooking at home?
3. What did you eat for breakfast today?
4. Do you prefer spicy food or sweet food?

### 🟡 Intermediate Questions
*Focus on explaining "why" and sharing stories.*

1. Can you recommend a good restaurant in Da Nang? Why do you like it?
2. How is the food in your home country different from Vietnamese food?
3. What is a dish you tried once but would never eat again?
4. Do you think street food is better than restaurant food?

### 🔴 Advanced Prompts
*Open-ended discussions.*

- Discuss how the diet and eating habits in your culture have changed over the past 20 years.
- "You are what you eat." Do you agree with this statement? How does food define culture?
