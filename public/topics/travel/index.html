Travel

Conversation practice about favorite places, future plans, and why certain trips matter.

Switch language

Use the matched page when the meetup swaps languages so the exercise stays intuitive for the next round.

Open Tiếng Việt

Topic Pack

Travel is reliable for mixed tables because locals can recommend places and visitors can compare experiences.

Use this at the meetup: each table takes these three patterns, speaks for 20 minutes, then rotates.

Patterns Used Today

Identity and Location [Subject] là người [Country] nhưng sống ở [Place] [Subject] is person [Country] but lives in [Place] Conditional Nếu [Condition], [Result] If [Condition], [Result] Opinions Theo [Person], [Statement] According to [Person], [Statement]

Example Prompts

  • Bạn là người nước nào nhưng sống ở đâu bây giờ? What country are you from, and where do you live now?
  • Nếu bạn có ba ngày nghỉ, bạn muốn đi đâu? If you had three days off, where would you want to go?
  • Theo bạn, nơi nào đẹp nhất ở miền Trung? In your opinion, what is the most beautiful place in Central Vietnam?
  • Bạn thích đi du lịch một mình hay với bạn bè hơn? Do you prefer traveling alone or with friends?

Vocabulary

du lịch travel địa điểm place or location bãi biển beach thành phố city khách sạn hotel đường đi route or way gần near xa far

Meetup Flow

  • Identity and Location
  • Conditional
  • Opinions

Rotate groups every 20 minutes. Run one Vietnamese-only round and one English-only round.

Host Prompts

  • Nếu có thêm thời gian thì sao? What about if you had more time?
  • Tại sao bạn chọn nơi đó? Why did you choose that place?
  • Có nơi nào bạn không đồng ý không? Is there any place you disagree about?
Pro tip

Bring a small notebook

  1. Read the topic page before the meetup.
  2. Copy the patterns into your notebook.
  3. Write three example sentences before you arrive.

You will speak more confidently during the exchange.
