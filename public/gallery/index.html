Friday, April 3, 2026 At An Hoi Cafe#

These photos are from the Friday, April 3, 2026 session at An Hoi Cafe. The night had a more intimate feel than the week before: around 12 people, a relaxed outdoor courtyard, and the kind of easy atmosphere that helps new members settle in fast.

What These Photos Show#

Relaxed night

Smaller turnout can be a strength

With around 12 people, everyone had more space to talk, rotate naturally, and settle into conversations without pressure.

New faces

Short notice, still a warm welcome

Even though the venue announcement came too late, the group still drew a strong mix of new and returning members and kept the energy friendly all evening.

Good service

Venue support changes the feel of the night

An Hoi Cafe staff were exceptionally helpful, and the owner offered a 10% discount, which made the night feel generous and easy to recommend.

Why These Matter#

  • They show the format is real, current, and active.
  • They help first-time members picture the room before they arrive.
  • They show that different venue styles can still support the same relaxed, learning-first meetup format.
