These cards are useful when a table needs a reset or when several new people arrive at once.

## Includes

- Friendly personal questions that work in both Vietnamese and English.
- Short follow-up prompts so conversations do not stop after one answer.
- A mix of beginner-safe and more open-ended questions for stronger tables.
