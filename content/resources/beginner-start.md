## What To Do With These Questions

Pick three questions before the meetup. Read them once or twice, then keep this page open on your phone.

At the table:

1. Ask one question slowly.
2. Listen for one or two familiar words in the answer.
3. Ask the next question.
4. Smile and keep going even if your pronunciation is not perfect.

## Good First Goal

Your goal is not to sound fluent. Your goal is to ask a few real questions and understand a little more than last time.
